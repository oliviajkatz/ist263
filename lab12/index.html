Solar System Planets

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun.
Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere and hottest surface.
Earth is our home planet with liquid water and life.
Mars is known as the Red Planet and may have once had water.
Jupiter is the largest planet with a Great Red Spot.
Saturn is famous for its bright ring system.
Uranus rotates on its side and has faint rings.
Neptune is blue and the windiest planet.
